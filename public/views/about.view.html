Solution Implemented

The structure of the championship is equal to a binary tree, each duel is formed by 2 players with their respective strategy, the winner of each duel moves to the next key and so on until a player wins.

Determinate the winner of a championship has many validations, first is necesary validate than the structure will be equal to binary tree, this mean that all player participates in exactly one match per round, also, is necesary validate the strategy of each player of the tournament, if strategy is diferent to R-P-S then throw an exception. If all structure is valid, all duels are played and then obtains the winner of the champioship. The result is stored in the database for have register of all winners and second position of the championships, all this with the purpose to get a TOP of best players.
